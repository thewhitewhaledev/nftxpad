Background

NFT is a digital asset based on blockchain technology that is currently popular in the crypto world. NFT offers great rewards for artists, creators and influencers from investors who are willing to pay for the NFT version of their digital assets.

Currently, many artists and creators do not understand how to integrate their artwork into digital assets based on blockchain technology.

Especially for artists and creators who have many artworks in the number of thousands of items that are ready to be integrated and made into digital assets in the form of NFT, this integration process is certainly not easy, requires large costs and takes a long time.

We are here to provide solutions by creating several services that make it easier for artists and creators to create NFTs as their digital assets secure, faster and cheaper.
